Capabilities

We are a multidisciplinary digital designer and developer, and turned freelance in 2012. From our studio in the India, we serve agencies, corporations and start-ups worldwide. We excel at integrating seamlessly into our clients’ teams, becoming their go-to contractor of choice.

We’ll fit seamlessly into your workflow. Whether that’s taking client feedback directly, or attending client meetings as ‘one of your agency team’, we never disclose who we are or what we do for your business.

Branding / Logo Design

We've had the privilege of working on some top brands, creating sub brands and giving start ups the help they need to create a professional identity.

UI & UX Design

Since 2012, We’ve designed streamlined, easy-to-use websites and user interfaces. We work to the principle that less is more, while carefully and strategically including important details and interactions.

Front End Development

We develop fast interactive front ends, using the latest technologies and techniques to reduce page load time. We use my knowledge and tools to save time in development, including preprocessors and our own custom framework to kick start a project.

Hire us

We usually book our available time in daily increments. A day is equal to 9.5 hours of work, which is also the minimum hours I usually book for depending on if we have worked together before or not. All time is required to be booked in advance. My standard working week is 47.5 hours

  • Rates : All work is billed on a day rate basis with consulting-only services at a fixed price. I always bill in ₹(INR) and all prices are plus GST if you are a Indian customer. If your project requires a fixed price then this is possible. My rates are non negotiable. My favourite introduction is.. "Hi Ram, We have a budget of X for X what could you do in that budget."
  • User Experience : For your online and mobile touch-points, we’ll create wireframes, user flows, information architecture, and interactive design.
  • Branding : Whether you’re looking to rebrand, or to develop brand guidelines and treatments, we can provide cost-effective inspiration and results.
  • Legal : All clients are required to sign a Contract of Works, this outlines the agreed hours, budget or fixed cost of the project as well as the scope and timeline. NDA agreements carry a fixed cost on top of any agreed hours. View a sample Contract of Works.
  • Availability : Please get in contact to check my current availability, this can change daily and usually book a few weeks in advance. Please still contact me if your project requires urgent attention.
